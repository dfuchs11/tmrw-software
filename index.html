The problem

Scheduling at independent facilities is still broken.

Most tools were built for enterprises or gyms with HR departments. Independent facility operators — the owner who teaches the classes, runs the front desk, and texts staff their schedule, all before noon — are still piecing it together with group chats and paper. TMRW fixes the entire loop.
